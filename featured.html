The Future of Blacks In Tech

The tech industry has long been a beacon of innovation, but it has also struggled with issues of diversity and inclusion. As we look to the future, the role of Blacks in tech is more important than ever. From increased representation to leadership roles, the journey is ongoing and filled with both challenges and opportunities.
